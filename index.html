WALZWERK Restaurant offers authentic and unique East German cuisine in the San Francisco Mission district since 1999. And we are still here for you in such difficult times!


We are using specific, all encompassing cleansing methods, allowing no one to work with any type of symptom as well as following all current CDC guidelines.

Delicious German Food

We serve delicious German food. Our dishes are authentic and traditional with a focus on recipes our parents and grand parents used to cook while living in former East Germany.

The beer, a german meaning

We offer a well crafted selection of German beers and wine to pair up with our delicious meals. Our East German favorites are: Köstritzer Schwarzbier and Radeberger Pilsner.

100% organic ingredients

All our vegetables are fresh, organic and local. Animals are raised without added hormones or antibiotics. Good for your health, the environment, and it also tastes better!
