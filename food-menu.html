Thank you for your support during covid 19

Dearest Walzwerk friends, 

We are still making delicious food! Come and pick some up, plus we have so many amazing bottles of German beer and wine!

Wednesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday   5:30pm - 8:30pm


How it works

  1. 1

    Call Walzwerk at 123456789 with a credit card to place your order 

  2. Drive up in front of Walzwerk

  3. Open your trunk or back window and we'll bring your food out with gloved hands and send you home with some delicious food!  You don't even have to get out of your car!  

  4. If you ordered delivery DoorDash (working on more companies, we’ll keep you posted). We will hand out your dinner (with gloved hands) to the delivery person. 


Our safety procedures to keep us all safe:

  1. All employees (Medardo, our chef + me, the owner) are checked before service every day

  2. All surfaces in kitchen and front of house are sanitized before using every time

  3. All employees sanitize our hands before doing any work

  4. All surfaces, doors and bathrooms are sanitized before, during and after service 

  5. All employees sanitize hands after touching anything anyone else has touched

Thank you so much for supporting Walzwerk during this trying time & please stay safe! oxoxo Christiane
